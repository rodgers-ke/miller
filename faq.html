Frequently Asked Questions

What courses do you offer?

We offer diploma and certificate programs in Hairdressing, Makeup Artistry, Nail Technology, Barbering, Skincare, and more.

Is the college accredited?

Yes, we are fully registered and accredited by relevant Kenyan authorities such as TVETA or NITA.

How do I apply?

You can apply online through our website or visit our campus. Walk-in applications are also welcome.

What are the entry requirements?

Most certificate courses require KCPE or KCSE. Some advanced programs may require prior experience.

When do intakes happen?

We have open intakes all year round. You can join at your convenience.

How much are the course fees?

Fees depend on the course. Please contact us or visit our website for the detailed fee structure.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we provide flexible payment plans either monthly or per term.

Do you provide practical training?

Yes! Practical hands-on training is included in all our programs using real salon equipment and client setups.

Will I get a certificate or diploma?

Yes. You will receive a recognized certificate or diploma upon successful completion of your course.

Do you offer job placement or internships?

Yes, we partner with salons and beauty brands to assist students with internships and job opportunities.

Can I study part-time or weekends?

Yes, we offer flexible schedules including part-time and weekend classes to suit your lifestyle.
